Why We Exist

We founded JSZ Caregiving with the belief that we all feel alone and overwhelmed sometimes, but with the support of people we feel comfortable and important around, we can all grow wings.

We support our DSPs with industry leading pay (~30% above average) and the freedom to choose the clients that they feel a connection with. And when they feel empowered to serve your family, you can feel reassured that they will do their best.

On paper, we employ DSPs to support individuals with developmental disabilities. But in our heart, we improve our employees’ lives so that they can pay it forward to you.

Our Approach

At JSZ Caregiving we believe that better care starts with better caregivers and ends with the kind of care that you want. And in between, it means trusting the same employee to show up everyday and commit to a relationship that lasts for years.
